# Robotics VLA Models Research

This research hub covers open-source Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for robotics, analyzing the four major factions competing to define the future of robot "brains."

## Overview

Major tech companies including Google, NVIDIA, Unitree, Ant Group, and Xiaomi have been releasing robot "brains" (VLA models) for free. This represents a strategic push to establish standards and build ecosystems in the robotics space.
