# Ant Group LingBot-VLA

LingBot-VLA is an open-source VLA model developed by Ant Group (蚂蚁集团), emphasizing cross-form generalization - the ability for "one brain to control all robot types."

## Key Approach

### Cross-Form Generalization

The core philosophy: **one model, many robot forms**

- Training data: 20,000+ hours of real robot data
- Robot diversity: 9 different dual-arm robot configurations
- Goal: A single brain capable of controlling any type of robot

This aligns with the "universal robot policy" vision similar to Octo, but with larger scale and Chinese development.

## Technical Focus

- Generalization across robot morphologies
- Real-world deployment capability
- Practical applications

## Company Background

Ant Group (蚂蚁集团):
- FinTech giant (Alipay parent company)
- Expanding into AI and robotics
- Brings commercial-scale development approach to research

## Strategic Position

Represents Chinese tech giants' entry into robotics AI:
- Leverages existing AI/ML expertise
- Applies practical development methodology
- Focuses on real-world deployment
